# Scrollytelling Story: The Rising Cost of Food

## Project Overview

This project explores **scrollytelling**, a narrative visualization technique where a story unfolds progressively as the reader scrolls through the page. Scrollytelling combines **data visualization, narrative structure, and interactive design** to create a more engaging way of communicating data-driven insights.

The story follows a student who returns home each week for his favorite dessert — **strawberry pie**. Through this narrative, the project illustrates how **rising food prices affect everyday experiences**.

As the reader scrolls through the story, data visualizations reveal trends in food prices and highlight the broader economic factors influencing grocery costs.

---

# Project Goals

The main objectives of this project were to:

* Explore **scrollytelling as a form of data storytelling**
* Combine **narrative storytelling with data visualization**
* Communicate economic trends in a **relatable and human-centered way**
* Demonstrate how storytelling techniques can make complex data more accessible

---

# Story Narrative

The narrative unfolds through several stages that gradually reveal the impact of rising food prices.

### Beginning: The Journey Home

The story introduces a student traveling home each week to enjoy a family tradition — strawberry pie. This section establishes the emotional context for the story.

---

### Middle: Rising Food Costs

As the narrative progresses, the reader learns that the ingredients used to make the pie are becoming increasingly expensive. Data visualizations illustrate trends in food prices and highlight the growing financial pressure on households.

---

### End: A Changing Tradition

The story concludes by reflecting on how broader economic changes can influence everyday experiences and traditions.

The narrative structure allows readers to connect emotionally with the story while also understanding the underlying data trends.

# Key Learning Outcomes

This project reinforced several important concepts related to **visual storytelling and data communication**.

### Storytelling Improves Engagement

Narrative-driven visualizations can make data more relatable and memorable for audiences.

### Sequential Presentation Guides Interpretation

Scrollytelling allows insights to be revealed progressively, helping audiences understand complex information step by step.

### Combining Narrative and Data Creates Impact

By connecting data trends with personal experiences, visualizations can communicate **both analytical insights and human impact**.

# Why Scrollytelling Matters

Scrollytelling has become a popular technique in **data journalism, media storytelling, and interactive reporting**. Major publications such as the New York Times frequently use scrollytelling to present complex topics in an accessible format.

This project demonstrates how scrollytelling can transform economic data into an engaging narrative that helps audiences better understand **real-world impacts of rising food prices**.
